1. Pays $200/month (revenue stream)
2. Submits gut DNA data (data asset building)
3. Returns every 6 months for retesting (longitudinal data points)
4. Stays for years because the product "improves over time"

Their own site says it explicitly: *"By banking your microbiome data and tracking its evolution against our growing database, we're building a longitudinal dataset that detects metabolic dysregulation years before it manifests clinically."*

They want to be the **Bloomberg Terminal of gut health** — the data layer that enables:

## Sex Health Data Angle (Key for Susan, 42F)

The `initialprompt.txt` explicitly mentions "sex health data." This is scientifically crucial:

- **Estrobolome**: The subset of gut bacteria that metabolize estrogen. Disruption = hormonal imbalance, PMS, perimenopause symptoms.
- Susan at 42 is likely **perimenopausal** — a period of major hormonal flux directly linked to gut microbiome changes.
- Female gut flora differs from male by composition AND function.
- Women's microbiome is modulated by: menstrual cycle, contraceptives, pregnancy history, hormonal therapy.
- Gut-hormone axis: bacteria produce/metabolize neurotransmitters (serotonin, GABA) AND sex hormones.
- **Female-specific bacteria**: Lactobacillus crispatus, Bifidobacterium longum levels track with estrogen.
